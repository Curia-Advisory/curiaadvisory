About Curia Advisory

Governance does not begin with policy. It begins with people who understand the law.

Curia Advisory is a dedicated AI Governance and Legal Technology advisory firm based in Harare, Zimbabwe. We exist because the regulatory landscape is moving faster than most organisations realise — and the legal obligations already in place are being consistently overlooked.

Zimbabwe's Cyber and Data Protection Act has been in force since 2021. The National AI Strategy 2026–2030 is now active. ZAIRA is forthcoming. The window between framework arrival and enforcement is closing. We help organisations use that window wisely.

Why Curia Advisory

The gap is not in the law. It is in who understands it.

Most organisations know AI is changing how they operate. Very few have mapped what that means for their legal obligations. The advisory capability to bridge that gap is rare — particularly in Zimbabwe, where the conversation is still forming.

Curia Advisory exists in that space. We bring multi-jurisdictional legal training, published expertise in AI governance, and a deep understanding of Zimbabwe's specific regulatory environment to every engagement.

  • 01
    Zimbabwe-specific expertise
    We know the CDPA, the National AI Strategy, and ZAIRA's forthcoming role. We do not apply international templates without adaptation.
  • 02
    International regulatory knowledge
    Our comparative training covers the EU AI Act, UNESCO Ethics Recommendation, OECD AI Principles, and South Africa's POPIA framework — and how they interact with Zimbabwe's instruments.
  • 03
    Published and peer-recognised
    Our analysis has been engaged by senior legal and governance professionals across Southern Africa. We publish, we research, and we contribute to the regional conversation.
  • 04
    Governance before the regulator arrives
    We help you build the right frameworks while you still have the time to do it properly — not in response to an enforcement action or a breach.
